标点符号是英文语法结构的一部分。同一串词语，标点不同，可能改变句意，也可能直接导致病句。第 3 章已经确立了"两个独立分句之间的合法连接"这条底线，本章在此基础上，按标点符号系统梳理每一个符号的用法，并给出结构与标点的对应总纲。

本章内容：

- 句末标点：句号、问号、感叹号
- 句内标点：逗号、分号、冒号
- 其他标点：破折号、括号、引号、省略号
- 撇号
- 结构与标点对应总纲
- 失误速查

---

## 6.1 句号（Period）

### 结束陈述句或祈使句

> ✅ She writes poems.  
> ✅ Please close the door.

### 用于缩写

> ✅ Dr. Smith, Mr. Brown, Ms. Lee  
> ✅ U.S.A., Ph.D., etc., i.e., e.g.

缩写出现在句末时，句号兼作句末标点，不重复：

> ✅ He earned his Ph.D.  
> ❌ He earned his Ph.D..

### 美式与英式差异

美式英语在缩写后加句号（Mr. / Dr. / U.S.A.），英式英语常省略（Mr / Dr / USA）。专四写作采用其中一种风格即可，保持全文一致。

---

## 6.2 问号（Question Mark）

### 结束直接疑问句

> ✅ Where are you going?  
> ✅ Did she finish the report?

### 间接疑问句不用问号

间接疑问句本质是陈述句，句末用句号：

> ❌ She asked where I was going?  
> ✅ She asked where I was going.

### 反意疑问句用问号

> ✅ You are a student, aren't you?

---

## 6.3 感叹号（Exclamation Point）

表达强烈情感。在学术与考试写作中使用频率极低，通常仅出现在直接引语中：

> ✅ "Watch out!" she shouted.

正式议论文中尽量避免使用感叹号。

---

## 6.4 逗号（Comma）

逗号是专四改错与写作中最容易出错的标点。用法归为七类。

### 用法一：分隔并列成分

连接三个及以上同类成分时，成分之间用逗号：

> ✅ She bought apples, oranges, and bananas.  
> ✅ The report is clear, concise, and informative.

最后一个成分前的逗号（牛津逗号，Oxford comma）可加可不加，全文保持一致即可。

### 用法二：逗号 + 并列连词连接独立分句

> ✅ It was raining, **so** we stayed home.  
> ✅ She studied hard, **but** she failed the exam.

两独立分句只有一个逗号而无并列连词，即为逗号粘连。详见[第 3 章](ch3-sentence-errors)。

### 用法三：分隔前置状语

前置的状语从句、状语短语或副词，与主句之间用逗号：

> ✅ **Because it was raining,** we stayed home.  
> ✅ **Walking along the river,** she felt peaceful.  
> ✅ **In the morning,** the streets were empty.  
> ✅ **However,** the plan failed.

状语后置时通常不加逗号：

> ✅ We stayed home because it was raining.  
> ✅ She felt peaceful walking along the river.

### 用法四：分隔非限定成分

非限定性定语从句、非限定性同位语、插入语两侧加逗号：

> ✅ My brother, **who lives in Beijing,** is a doctor.  
> ✅ Beijing, **the capital of China,** has a long history.  
> ✅ The plan, **in my opinion,** is feasible.

限定性成分不加逗号：

> ✅ My brother who lives in Beijing is a doctor.　（暗示有多个兄弟）

两者的语义差异详见[第 5 章](ch5-subordination)。

### 用法五：分隔直接引语与引述动词

> ✅ She said, "I will come."  
> ✅ "I will come," she said.  
> ✅ "I will come," she said, "if the weather permits."

### 用法六：分隔日期、地址、数字、称呼

> ✅ July 4, 1776  
> ✅ She lives in Paris, France.  
> ✅ 1,234,567  
> ✅ Dear John,

### 用法七：分隔并列形容词

当多个形容词地位平等时，用逗号分隔：

> ✅ a long, tedious, exhausting journey

判断方法：若形容词之间可以插入 `and`，或可以调换顺序而不影响语义，就是并列形容词，用逗号。否则不用。

> ✅ a **beautiful red** dress　（不可说 "red and beautiful dress"，不加逗号）  
> ✅ a **beautiful, elegant** dress　（可说 "beautiful and elegant dress"，加逗号）

### 逗号不应出现的位置

**主语与谓语之间**

> ❌ The student who sat in the front row, got the highest score.  
> ✅ The student who sat in the front row got the highest score.

**谓语与宾语之间**

> ❌ She said, that she would come.  
> ✅ She said that she would come.

（引导宾语从句的 `that` 前不加逗号。直接引语的 `that` 替换为引号时才加逗号。）

**两个并列成分之间**

> ❌ She speaks English, and French.  
> ✅ She speaks English and French.

（两成分并列不加逗号；三成分以上才加。）

## 6.5 分号（Semicolon）

### 用法一：连接两个语义紧密的独立分句

> ✅ It was raining; we stayed home.  
> ✅ Some students prefer morning classes; others prefer evening ones.

分号两侧必须都是独立分句。若有一侧不是，即为误用：

> ❌ It was raining; because the typhoon arrived.  
> ✅ It was raining because the typhoon arrived.

### 用法二：配合连接副词

> ✅ It was raining; **however,** we went out.  
> ✅ He studied hard; **therefore,** he passed.

详见[第 4 章](ch4-coordination)。

### 用法三：分隔内部含逗号的并列项

当并列项自身包含逗号时，用分号代替外层逗号，以免混淆：

> ✅ The conference will be held in Beijing, China; Tokyo, Japan; and Seoul, South Korea.  
> ✅ The committee includes Dr. Smith, the chairman; Ms. Lee, the secretary; and Mr. Brown, the treasurer.

---

## 6.6 冒号（Colon）

### 用法一：引出列表

冒号之前必须是**完整的独立分句**：

> ✅ She bought three items: apples, oranges, and bananas.  
> ❌ She bought: apples, oranges, and bananas.  
> ✅ She bought apples, oranges, and bananas.

### 用法二：引出解释或举例

> ✅ The reason is simple: nobody cared.  
> ✅ He had one goal: to win.

### 用法三：引出正式引语或长引语

> ✅ The professor made one point clear: "Plagiarism will not be tolerated."

### 用法四：固定场合

> ✅ Dear Professor Smith:　（正式信件称呼）  
> ✅ 9:30 a.m.　（时间）  
> ✅ Genesis 1:1　（书卷章节）

### 冒号后的首字母大小写

冒号后若为独立分句，美式英语常首字母大写，英式英语常小写。若为短语或列表，小写。专四写作保持全文一致即可。

---

## 6.7 破折号（Em Dash）与括号（Parentheses）

### 破折号

在句中插入强调性的补充信息。两侧各一个破折号，不与逗号或句号并用：

> ✅ The answer—if there is one—lies in the data.  
> ✅ Three students—Alice, Bob, and Carol—won the prize.

破折号在正式学术写作中使用频率低于逗号和括号。

### 括号

在句中插入次要的补充信息。括号内的内容可删除而不影响主句语法：

> ✅ The novel (published in 1925) became a classic.  
> ✅ Several cities (Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou) are on the list.

括号内若为完整独立分句且独立成句，句号置于括号内；若嵌入在主句中，主句的句号置于右括号之后：

> ✅ She finally arrived. (The flight had been delayed for hours.)  
> ✅ She finally arrived (after hours of delay).

### 三者对比

|标点|强调程度|正式程度|
|---|---|---|
|逗号|中|高|
|破折号|高|中|
|括号|低|高|

---

## 6.8 引号（Quotation Marks）

### 用法一：直接引语

> ✅ She said, "I will come tomorrow."  
> ✅ "I will come tomorrow," she said.

### 用法二：标示术语或特殊用法

> ✅ The word "literally" is often misused.

### 用法三：标示短篇作品标题

短篇作品（文章、诗歌、短篇小说、歌曲、剧集单集）标题用引号；长篇作品（书籍、报刊、电影、专辑、剧集）标题用斜体。详见[第 7 章](ch7-mechanics)。

> ✅ The article "The Future of AI" appeared in _The Economist_.

### 美式与英式的引号用法

**美式**使用双引号 `"..."`，引号内再嵌套时用单引号 `'...'`。句末的句号和逗号**置于引号内**：

> ✅ She said, "I'll come."  
> ✅ He called it "a masterpiece," though I disagree.

**英式**常用单引号 `'...'`，嵌套时用双引号。句末标点置于引号外，除非该标点属于引语本身。

专四写作采用美式用法即可。

### 问号与感叹号的位置

问号或感叹号属于引语本身时，置于引号内；属于主句时，置于引号外：

> ✅ She asked, "Are you coming?"　（问号属于引语）  
> ✅ Did she say, "I will come"?　（问号属于主句）

---

## 6.9 撇号（Apostrophe）

### 用法一：所有格

单数名词加 `'s`：

> ✅ the student's book  
> ✅ James's car　（现代美式）/ James' car　（传统用法，两者皆可）

以 `s` 结尾的复数名词只加撇号：

> ✅ the students' books  
> ✅ the workers' rights

不以 `s` 结尾的复数名词加 `'s`：

> ✅ the children's toys  
> ✅ the women's conference

### 用法二：缩写

> ✅ it's = it is / it has  
> ✅ don't = do not  
> ✅ '90s = 1990s

### its 与 it's

两者常被混淆：

|形式|含义|例|
|---|---|---|
|its|物主代词"它的"|The cat licked **its** paws.|
|it's|it is / it has 的缩写|**It's** raining.|

> ❌ The company lost it's reputation.  
> ✅ The company lost its reputation.

### 复数不加撇号

普通名词变复数时只加 `s` 或 `es`，不加撇号：

> ❌ She bought three book's.  
> ✅ She bought three books.

---

## 6.10 省略号（Ellipsis）

表示省略的词句：

> ✅ "The report was... inconclusive," she admitted.

在引用文献时，省略号用于标示被省略的原文部分。正式写作中谨慎使用。

---

## 6.11 结构与标点对应总纲

下表汇总各类句法结构对应的合法标点形式，是本章的核心参考。

|结构关系|合法标点形式|示例|
|---|---|---|
|两独立分句分立|句号|It rained. We stayed home.|
|两独立分句语义紧密|分号|It rained; we stayed home.|
|两独立分句 + 并列连词|逗号 + FANBOYS|It rained, so we stayed home.|
|两独立分句 + 连接副词|分号 + 副词 + 逗号|It rained; therefore, we stayed home.|
|状语从句前置|逗号|Because it rained, we stayed home.|
|状语从句后置|无标点|We stayed home because it rained.|
|分词短语前置|逗号|Walking home, she felt tired.|
|分词短语后置（状语）|逗号|She walked home, feeling tired.|
|非限定性定语从句|两侧加逗号|My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor.|
|限定性定语从句|不加逗号|My brother who lives in Beijing is a doctor.|
|非限定性同位语|两侧加逗号|Beijing, the capital of China, has a long history.|
|直接引语|引号 + 逗号|She said, "I will come."|
|列表|冒号（前接完整句）|She bought three items: apples, oranges, and bananas.|
|解释或举例|冒号|The reason is simple: nobody cared.|
|并列成分（三个以上）|逗号|apples, oranges, and bananas|
|内部含逗号的并列项|分号|Beijing, China; Tokyo, Japan; Seoul, South Korea|
|强调性补充|两侧加破折号|The answer—if there is one—lies in the data.|
|次要补充|括号|The novel (published in 1925) became a classic.|

## 6.12 失误速查

|错例|问题|合法改写|
|---|---|---|
|She opened the door, a cat ran in.|逗号粘连|She opened the door**, and** a cat ran in. / She opened the door**;** a cat ran in.|
|He studied hard, therefore, he passed.|连接副词前应用分号|He studied hard**;** therefore, he passed.|
|It was raining; because the typhoon arrived.|分号后非独立分句|It was raining because the typhoon arrived.|
|She bought: apples, oranges, and bananas.|冒号前非独立分句|She bought apples, oranges, and bananas. / She bought three items: apples, oranges, and bananas.|
|The student who sat in the front row, got the highest score.|主谓之间不应有逗号|The student who sat in the front row got the highest score.|
|She said, that she would come.|宾语从句 that 前不加逗号|She said that she would come.|
|My brother, that lives in Beijing, is a doctor.|非限定性定语从句不可用 that|My brother, **who** lives in Beijing, is a doctor.|
|The company lost it's reputation.|its 误作 it's|The company lost **its** reputation.|
|She asked where I was going?|间接疑问句不用问号|She asked where I was going**.**|
|He earned his Ph.D..|句末缩写句号不重复|He earned his Ph.D.|
|She said "I will come."|引述动词后缺逗号|She said**,** "I will come."|
|She bought apples, oranges and, bananas.|牛津逗号位置错误|She bought apples, oranges**,** and bananas. / She bought apples, oranges and bananas.|
