    <div class="swiss-section-body">
      <h2 id="chapters-title">七项知识</h2>
      <div class="chapter-table">
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">01</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch1-sentence-skeleton">句子骨架</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">五种基本句型 SV / SVC / SVO / SVOO / SVOC，以及 There be 结构与倒装。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">句子不完整、缺谓语、系动词后误用副词。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">02</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch2-subject-verb-agreement">主谓一致</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">按主语类型归纳判断方法：并列主语、集合名词、不定代词、数量短语、名词性从句、there be 结构。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">改错题高频考点。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">03</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch3-sentence-errors">三类致命句子错误</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">破句（Fragment）、逗号粘连（Comma Splice）、粘连句（Run-on），以及连接独立分句的四种合法方式。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">改错与写作双重高频扣分点。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">04</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch4-coordination">并列</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">FANBOYS、关联连词、连接副词的辨析；平行结构（parallelism）原则。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">平行失衡、连接副词当并列连词用。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">05</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch5-subordination">从属</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">名词性从句、定语从句、状语从句；非谓语短语；垂悬修饰语的识别与修改。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">垂悬分词、although 与 but 连用。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">06</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch6-punctuation">标点符号</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">句末与句内标点；结构与标点的对应总纲。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">逗号滥用、分号误用、冒号前非完整句。</div>
        </article>
        <article class="chapter-row">
          <div class="chapter-no">07</div>
          <div class="chapter-title"><a href="ch7-mechanics">卷面规范</a></div>
          <div class="chapter-focus">拼写、大小写、数字、缩写、斜体、连字符。</div>
          <div class="chapter-risk">写作评分中直接计入扣分的细节。</div>
        </article>
      </div>
