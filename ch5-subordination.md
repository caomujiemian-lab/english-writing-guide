**从属**（subordination）把多条信息分出主次：核心命题保留在主句，次级信息降级为**从句**或**短语**。第 4 章的并列处理同级信息，本章处理层级信息。一个专四级长句之所以能"长"，主要靠的就是从属。

本章内容：

- 从属的两种形式：从句与短语
- 三类从句：名词性、定语、状语
- 四类非谓语短语
- 垂悬修饰语
- Although / Because 与 but / so 连用
- 失误速查

---

## 5.1 从属的两种形式

从属结构有两种承载形式：

|形式|特征|例|
|---|---|---|
|**从句**（subordinate clause）|含主语与谓语，由从属连词或关系词引导|**Although it was raining,** we went out.|
|**短语**（phrase）|不含限定谓语，由非谓语动词或介词引导|**Despite the rain,** we went out.|

两种形式可互相改写。例如下面两句语义相同，结构不同：

> ✅ **Because she was tired,** she went to bed early.　（状语从句）  
> ✅ **Feeling tired,** she went to bed early.　（分词短语）

从句结构清晰、信息完整；短语结构紧凑、节奏更快。考试长句中两者常同时出现。

## 5.2 名词性从句

**名词性从句**（noun clause）在主句中充当名词成分：主语、宾语、表语、同位语。

### 引导词

|引导词类别|成员|是否在从句中充当成分|
|---|---|---|
|从属连词|that, whether, if|不充当成分|
|连接代词|who, whom, whose, what, which|充当主语、宾语、定语|
|连接副词|when, where, why, how|充当状语|

### 四种位置

**主语从句**

> ✅ **That she passed the exam** surprised everyone.  
> ✅ **It** surprised everyone **that she passed the exam**.

主语从句较长时，常用 `it` 作形式主语，真正的主语从句后置。

**宾语从句**

> ✅ I believe **that he is telling the truth**.  
> ✅ She asked **whether I had finished the report**.

作动词宾语时，`that` 可省略；`whether` / `if` 不可省略。

**表语从句**

> ✅ The question is **whether we should accept the offer**.  
> ✅ His concern is **that the deadline is too tight**.

**同位语从句**

> ✅ The fact **that she lied** shocked her parents.  
> ✅ The belief **that hard work leads to success** motivates them.

同位语从句说明前面抽象名词（fact, idea, news, belief, hope, question 等）的具体内容。

### whether 与 if 的区别

两者都可引导宾语从句表示"是否"，但：

|用法|whether|if|
|---|---|---|
|引导主语从句|✅|❌|
|引导表语从句|✅|❌|
|引导同位语从句|✅|❌|
|作介词宾语|✅|❌|
|后接 or not|✅（whether or not）|❌|
|引导动词宾语从句|✅|✅|

> ❌ **If** she will come is still unknown.  
> ✅ **Whether** she will come is still unknown.

> ❌ The question is **if** we should leave.  
> ✅ The question is **whether** we should leave.

### that 作引导词 vs that 作关系词

`that` 引导名词性从句时**不在从句中充当成分**，只起连接作用；引导定语从句时**在从句中充当成分**（主语或宾语）。

> ✅ I know **that** she is honest.　（that 无成分，名词性从句）  
> ✅ The book **that** I borrowed is fascinating.　（that 作 borrowed 的宾语，定语从句）

## 5.3 定语从句

**定语从句**（relative clause）修饰一个名词（先行词），由**关系代词**或**关系副词**引导。

### 关系词

|关系词|先行词|在从句中的成分|
|---|---|---|
|who|人|主语|
|whom|人|宾语|
|whose|人 / 物|定语|
|which|物|主语或宾语|
|that|人 / 物|主语或宾语|
|when|时间名词|时间状语|
|where|地点名词|地点状语|
|why|reason|原因状语|

### 限定性 vs 非限定性

|类型|功能|标点|关系词限制|
|---|---|---|---|
|**限定性**（restrictive）|缩小先行词范围，不可删除|不加逗号|who/whom/which/that 皆可|
|**非限定性**（non-restrictive）|补充说明，可删除|两侧加逗号|不可用 that|

> ✅ My brother **who lives in Beijing** is a doctor.  
> ✅ My brother**, who lives in Beijing,** is a doctor.

第一句暗示"我"有多个兄弟，这个定语从句用来区分哪一个；第二句暗示"我"只有一个兄弟，从句只是补充说明。两句的语义完全不同。

> ❌ My brother, **that** lives in Beijing, is a doctor.  
> ✅ My brother, **who** lives in Beijing, is a doctor.

非限定性定语从句不可用 `that`。

### 关系代词的省略

限定性定语从句中，关系代词作**宾语**时可省略；作**主语**时不可省略。

> ✅ The book **(that) I borrowed** is fascinating.　（that 是 borrowed 的宾语，可省略）  
> ✅ The book **that is on the desk** is mine.　（that 是主语，不可省略）

### 关系副词与"介词 + which"

表示时间、地点、原因的关系副词可改写为"介词 + which"：

> ✅ The day **when** she arrived was sunny.  
> ✅ The day **on which** she arrived was sunny.

> ✅ The city **where** I grew up has changed.  
> ✅ The city **in which** I grew up has changed.

> ✅ The reason **why** he left is unclear.  
> ✅ The reason **for which** he left is unclear.

### 介词的位置

"介词 + 关系代词"结构中，介词可前置也可后置。前置时不可用 `that`，关系代词作人时用 `whom`（不用 who）：

> ✅ The man **to whom** I spoke is the manager.  
> ✅ The man **(that/who/whom) I spoke to** is the manager.  
> ❌ The man **to who** I spoke is the manager.  
> ❌ The man **to that** I spoke is the manager.

## 5.4 状语从句

**状语从句**（adverbial clause）修饰主句的动词、形容词或整句，由**从属连词**引导。按逻辑关系分类：

|关系|常用从属连词|
|---|---|
|时间|when, while, as, before, after, since, until, as soon as, once|
|地点|where, wherever|
|原因|because, since, as, now that|
|结果|so ... that, such ... that, so that|
|目的|so that, in order that, lest|
|条件|if, unless, provided (that), as long as|
|让步|although, though, even though, even if, while, whereas|
|方式|as, as if, as though|
|比较|than, as ... as|

### 状语从句的位置与标点

状语从句前置时，与主句之间加逗号；后置时，通常不加逗号。

> ✅ **Because it was raining,** we stayed home.  
> ✅ We stayed home **because it was raining**.

详见[第 6 章 标点符号](ch6-punctuation)。

### 时态配合

主句为过去时，时间、条件、让步状语从句一般使用相应的过去时；在**表示将来**的语境下，时间和条件状语从句用**一般现在时**代替将来时。

> ✅ **When she arrives,** I will call you.　（而非 When she will arrive）  
> ✅ **If it rains tomorrow,** the game will be cancelled.　（而非 If it will rain）

### 特殊句式

**so ... that / such ... that**

> ✅ The coffee was **so hot that** I couldn't drink it.  
> ✅ It was **such a hot day that** we stayed indoors.

`so` 后接形容词或副词；`such` 后接名词短语。

**the more ... the more ...**

> ✅ **The harder** you work, **the more** you achieve.

## 5.5 四类非谓语短语

**非谓语动词**（non-finite verb）不能独立作谓语，但可以构成短语作定语、状语、主语、宾语、表语。四种形式：

|形式|主动|被动|
|---|---|---|
|不定式（infinitive）|to do|to be done|
|动名词（gerund）|doing|being done|
|现在分词（present participle）|doing|being done|
|过去分词（past participle）|—|done|

### 不定式短语

作目的状语、结果状语、后置定语、主语、宾语、宾语补语。

> ✅ She went to the library **to borrow a book**.　（目的状语）  
> ✅ He is the person **to ask**.　（后置定语）  
> ✅ **To finish the project on time** requires teamwork.　（主语）

### 动名词短语

作主语、宾语、表语、介词宾语。

> ✅ **Swimming every morning** keeps her healthy.　（主语）  
> ✅ She enjoys **reading poetry**.　（宾语）  
> ✅ He is good at **solving problems**.　（介词宾语）

### 分词短语

**现在分词短语**表主动或进行：

> ✅ **Walking along the river,** she felt peaceful.　（伴随状语）  
> ✅ The man **standing by the window** is my uncle.　（后置定语）

**过去分词短语**表被动或完成：

> ✅ **Written in plain English,** the book sold well.　（原因状语）  
> ✅ The book **written by Hemingway** became a classic.　（后置定语）

### 独立主格结构

当分词短语的逻辑主语与主句主语不同时，在分词前保留其逻辑主语，构成**独立主格结构**（absolute construction）。

> ✅ **The weather being fine,** we decided to go hiking.  
> ✅ **All things considered,** the plan is feasible.  
> ✅ **She stood at the door, her eyes filled with tears.**

## 5.6 垂悬修饰语

**垂悬修饰语**（dangling modifier）指分词短语、不定式短语或介词短语的**逻辑主语**与主句主语**不一致**，导致修饰关系混乱。

### 典型错误

> ❌ **Walking along the street,** a dog bit me.

`Walking` 的逻辑主语应是"在街上走的人"，但主句主语是 `a dog`，形成"狗在街上走"的荒唐语义。

> ❌ **To improve English,** many books are read.

`To improve` 的逻辑主语应是某个人，但主句主语是 `many books`，形成"书为了提高英语而被读"的语义错位。

> ❌ **Having finished the exam,** the classroom was empty.

`Having finished` 的逻辑主语应是考完试的学生，但主句主语是 `the classroom`。

### 修改方向

**方向一：调整主句主语，使其与分词的逻辑主语一致**

> ✅ **Walking along the street,** I was bitten by a dog.  
> ✅ **To improve English,** she reads many books.  
> ✅ **Having finished the exam,** the students left the classroom.

**方向二：把分词短语改写为状语从句，显性补出逻辑主语**

> ✅ **While I was walking along the street,** a dog bit me.  
> ✅ **In order for students to improve English,** many books should be read.  
> ✅ **After the students finished the exam,** the classroom was empty.

### 判定方法

在分词短语后紧接着问一句"Who / What + 分词动作"，答案如果与紧随其后的主句主语不同，就是垂悬修饰语。

> `Walking along the street, a dog bit me.`  
> Who is walking? → the speaker  
> 主句主语 → a dog  
> 不一致 → 垂悬

## 5.7 Although / Because 与 but / so 连用

汉语"虽然……但是……"和"因为……所以……"是两个连词同现，英语中只能保留其中一个。

> ❌ **Although** he is rich, **but** he is not happy.  
> ✅ **Although** he is rich, he is not happy.  
> ✅ He is rich, **but** he is not happy.

> ❌ **Because** it was raining, **so** we stayed home.  
> ✅ **Because** it was raining, we stayed home.  
> ✅ It was raining, **so** we stayed home.

错误根源在于混淆了**从属**（although / because 引导从句）与**并列**（but / so 连接分句）两种结构。一个复合句只能选择其中一种。

同类错误还出现在 though / even though / even if 上：

> ❌ **Even though** she tried her best, **but** she failed.  
> ✅ **Even though** she tried her best, she failed.

## 5.8 失误速查

|错例|问题|合法改写|
|---|---|---|
|If she will come is still unknown.|主语从句不可用 if|**Whether** she will come is still unknown.|
|My brother, that lives in Beijing, is a doctor.|非限定性定语从句不可用 that|My brother, **who** lives in Beijing, is a doctor.|
|The man to who I spoke is the manager.|介词前置后应用 whom|The man **to whom** I spoke is the manager.|
|When she will arrive, I will call you.|时间状语从句中用一般现在时代替将来时|**When she arrives,** I will call you.|
|The coffee was so a hot drink that I couldn't drink it.|so 后不接名词短语|The coffee was **such a hot drink that** I couldn't drink it.|
|Walking along the street, a dog bit me.|垂悬修饰语|**Walking along the street, I was bitten by a dog.**|
|To improve English, many books are read.|垂悬修饰语|**To improve English, she reads many books.**|
|The weather was fine, we decided to go hiking.|独立主格被误作独立分句|**The weather being fine,** we decided to go hiking.|
|Although he is rich, but he is not happy.|although 与 but 连用|**Although** he is rich, he is not happy.|
|Because it was raining, so we stayed home.|because 与 so 连用|**Because** it was raining, we stayed home.|
|The fact which she lied shocked her parents.|同位语从句应用 that|The fact **that** she lied shocked her parents.|
|I asked him that he had finished.|疑问宾语从句应用 whether/if|I asked him **whether/if** he had finished.|
