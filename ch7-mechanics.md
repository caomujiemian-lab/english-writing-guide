卷面规范指拼写、大小写、数字、缩写、斜体、连字符等细节。这些规则不影响句子的语法结构，但在专四写作评分中直接计入扣分项。一个语法完全正确的句子，若出现 `english` 或 `recieve` 这类错误，同样扣分。

## 7.1 拼写规则

英语拼写有若干成文规则，覆盖了大部分常用词的变形。

### 规则一：i before e, except after c

> ✅ believe, achieve, relieve, niece, piece  
> ✅ receive, deceive, ceiling, perceive

发音为 /iː/ 时，`i` 在 `e` 之前；若前面是 `c`，则 `e` 在 `i` 之前。

例外：发音不是 /iː/ 时，此规则不适用：

> ✅ neighbor, weigh, eight, height, their, foreign

### 规则二：词尾不发音的 e

以不发音的 `e` 结尾的词，后接**以元音开头的后缀**时，去掉 `e`；后接**以辅音开头的后缀**时，保留 `e`。

|原词|+ 元音后缀|+ 辅音后缀|
|---|---|---|
|hope|hoping|hopeful|
|care|caring|careful|
|use|using, usable|useless, useful|
|love|loving, lovable|lovely|

例外：为保留 `c` 或 `g` 的软音，`e` 须保留：

> ✅ notice → noticeable  
> ✅ courage → courageous  
> ✅ manage → manageable

### 规则三：词尾 y 的变化

以"辅音 + y"结尾的词，后接后缀时，`y` 变 `i`（但后缀为 `-ing` 时 `y` 保留）：

|原词|变形|
|---|---|
|study|studies, studied, studying|
|happy|happier, happiest, happily, happiness|
|try|tries, tried, trying|
|beauty|beautiful, beauties|

以"元音 + y"结尾的词，`y` 保留：

> ✅ play → plays, played, playing  
> ✅ enjoy → enjoys, enjoyed, enjoyment

### 规则四：双写末尾辅音

以"单元音 + 单辅音"结尾的**单音节词**，或**重音在末音节**的多音节词，后接以元音开头的后缀时，双写末尾辅音：

|原词|变形|
|---|---|
|stop|stopped, stopping|
|plan|planned, planning|
|begin|beginning, beginner|
|prefer|preferred, preferring|
|occur|occurred, occurring|

重音不在末音节时不双写：

> ✅ visit → visited, visiting　（重音在首音节）  
> ✅ open → opened, opening  
> ✅ offer → offered, offering

### 规则五：-ic 结尾词加 -ally

以 `-ic` 结尾的形容词，副词形式加 `-ally`（而非 `-ly`）：

> ✅ basic → basically  
> ✅ automatic → automatically  
> ✅ specific → specifically  
> ✅ dramatic → dramatically

唯一常见例外：

> ✅ public → publicly

### 规则六：复数形式

|规则|示例|
|---|---|
|一般加 -s|book → books|
|以 s, x, z, sh, ch 结尾加 -es|box → boxes, dish → dishes|
|辅音 + y，y 变 i 加 -es|story → stories|
|元音 + y，直接加 -s|boy → boys|
|以 f/fe 结尾，变 ves|leaf → leaves, wife → wives|
|以 o 结尾，情况不一|potato → potatoes, photo → photos|
|不规则复数|child → children, man → men, foot → feet|
|单复数同形|sheep, deer, fish, species|

## 7.2 易混淆的同音异形词

拼写检查工具无法识别语境错误，这类错误在专四作文中频繁出现。

|词组|含义与词性|例|
|---|---|---|
|their / there / they're|他们的 / 那里 / they are|**Their** house is over **there**, and **they're** happy.|
|its / it's|它的 / it is 或 it has|The cat licked **its** paws. **It's** raining.|
|your / you're|你的 / you are|**Your** book is here. **You're** welcome.|
|whose / who's|谁的 / who is 或 who has|**Whose** book is this? **Who's** at the door?|
|to / too / two|介词 / 也 / 二|She went **to** the store **to** buy **two** apples. I want to go **too**.|
|then / than|然后 / 比|She is taller **than** I am. First we ate, **then** we left.|
|affect / effect|影响（动词）/ 影响（名词）|The weather **affects** my mood. The **effect** was immediate.|
|accept / except|接受 / 除……之外|She **accepted** the offer. Everyone came **except** John.|
|advice / advise|建议（名词）/ 建议（动词）|He gave me good **advice**. I **advise** you to stay.|
|principal / principle|校长、主要的 / 原则|The **principal** reason is clear. She sticks to her **principles**.|
|lose / loose|失去 / 松的|Don't **lose** your keys. The screw is **loose**.|
|complement / compliment|补充 / 赞美|The wine **complements** the meal. She gave me a **compliment**.|
|stationary / stationery|静止的 / 文具|The car remained **stationary**. She bought **stationery** at the shop.|
|site / sight / cite|地点 / 视力 / 引用|The construction **site** is out of **sight**. She **cited** three sources.|

## 7.3 大小写

### 大写规则

**句首首字母**

> ✅ She arrived on time.

**专有名词**：具体的人名、地名、机构名、国名、民族、语言、宗教、品牌等。

> ✅ Albert Einstein, Beijing, Harvard University  
> ✅ China, Chinese, English, French  
> ✅ Christianity, Buddhism, Islam  
> ✅ Apple, Google, Microsoft

**第一人称代词 I**

> ✅ My friend and **I** went shopping.

**月份、星期、节日**

> ✅ January, Monday, Christmas, National Day

**作品标题**：首词、末词、实词全部大写，冠词、介词、并列连词（三字母及以下）一般小写。

> ✅ _The Catcher in the Rye_  
> ✅ "Of Mice and Men"  
> ✅ _Pride and Prejudice_

**课程具体名称与学位**

> ✅ She is taking History 101.  
> ✅ He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree.

**引语首词**（直接引语）

> ✅ She said, "**W**e are leaving soon."

### 不大写的情形

**季节**

> ✅ spring, summer, autumn, winter

**学科一般名称**（非专有名词化）

> ✅ She studies history, biology, and mathematics.  
> ✅ She studies English, French, and Chinese.（语言名大写）

**方位词作方向**

> ✅ Drive **north** for two miles.　（方向不大写）  
> ✅ She grew up in the **North**.　（特定区域大写）

## 7.4 数字

### 拼写 vs 阿拉伯数字

**拼写**：一位到两位数的普通数字、句首数字、近似数。

> ✅ She has **three** brothers.  
> ✅ **Twenty** students attended.  
> ✅ About **five hundred** people came.

**阿拉伯数字**：精确数据、百分比、日期、时间、页码、章节、金额、度量单位。

> ✅ The population reached **1.4 billion**.  
> ✅ The success rate is **85%**.  
> ✅ She was born on **July 4, 1995**.  
> ✅ The meeting starts at **9:30 a.m.**  
> ✅ See page **237**.

### 句首数字一律拼写

> ❌ **15** students passed the exam.  
> ✅ **Fifteen** students passed the exam.  
> ✅ A total of **15** students passed the exam.

若数字过大难以拼写，改写句子以避免数字出现在句首。

### 同句中保持一致

同一句中同类数字应采用同一形式：

> ❌ The class has **twelve** boys and **15** girls.  
> ✅ The class has **12** boys and **15** girls.  
> ✅ The class has **twelve** boys and **fifteen** girls.

### 连字符与数字

21 到 99 的拼写数字用连字符：

> ✅ twenty-one, forty-five, ninety-nine  
> ✅ one hundred and twenty-three

## 7.5 缩写

### 可缩写的情形

**头衔与学位**

> ✅ Dr. Smith, Prof. Lee, Mr. Brown, Ph.D., M.A., B.A.

**知名机构与国家**（首次出现时写全称，括注缩写；此后使用缩写）

> ✅ The World Health Organization (WHO) issued a statement. The WHO later clarified...  
> ✅ The United Nations (UN) passed the resolution.

**计量单位**

> ✅ 10 kg, 5 cm, 100 km/h

**拉丁缩写**

|缩写|原文|含义|用法|
|---|---|---|---|
|e.g.|exempli gratia|例如|引出举例|
|i.e.|id est|即、也就是|引出解释|
|etc.|et cetera|等等|列表末尾|
|et al.|et alii|及其他人|学术引用多作者|
|cf.|confer|比较|参考对照|
|vs.|versus|对、相对|对立比较|

> ✅ Citrus fruits (e.g., oranges, lemons) are rich in vitamin C.  
> ✅ She studies linguistics, i.e., the scientific study of language.

### 正式写作中不宜缩写的情形

**国家名、月份、星期在正文中写全称**

> ❌ She moved to the U.K. in Dec.  
> ✅ She moved to the United Kingdom in December.

（地址、脚注、表格中的缩写除外。）

**口语缩略形式在正式写作中展开**

> ❌ I **don't** think it **can't** work.  
> ✅ I **do not** think it **cannot** work.

考试作文评分中，`don't` / `can't` / `won't` 等缩略形式可能被视作非正式，展开写更稳妥。

### 缩写的句末标点

缩写自带句号且出现在句末时，句号不重复：

> ✅ The meeting starts at 9 a.m.  
> ❌ The meeting starts at 9 a.m..

## 7.6 斜体

考试手写中以**下划线**代替斜体。

### 用法一：长篇作品标题

|使用斜体|使用引号|
|---|---|
|书籍|文章|
|报纸、杂志|短篇小说|
|电影、电视剧|剧集单集、歌曲|
|音乐专辑|诗歌（短篇）|
|戏剧、长诗|章节标题|

> ✅ I read _Pride and Prejudice_ last week.  
> ✅ The article "The Future of AI" appeared in _The Economist_.  
> ✅ She watched _Friends_ last night, especially the episode "The One with the Thanksgiving Flashbacks."

### 用法二：外来词

尚未完全融入英语的外来词用斜体：

> ✅ The concept of _Bildungsroman_ originated in German literature.  
> ✅ She has a certain _je ne sais quoi_.

已广泛使用、收入词典的外来词不用斜体：

> ✅ The cafe serves excellent pasta.  
> ✅ She hosted the event.

### 用法三：强调

> ✅ She did _not_ say that.

正式写作中慎用，过度强调会削弱效果。

### 用法四：词语本身作为讨论对象

> ✅ The word _literally_ is often misused.  
> ✅ _Affect_ and _effect_ are frequently confused.

### 用法五：船只、飞机、航天器专名

> ✅ the _Titanic_, the _Apollo 11_, the USS _Enterprise_

## 7.7 连字符

### 用法一：复合定语在名词前

两个及以上的词组合修饰一个名词，放在名词前时用连字符连接；放在名词后则不用。

> ✅ a **well-known** author  
> ✅ The author is **well known**.

> ✅ a **ten-year-old** boy  
> ✅ The boy is **ten years old**.

> ✅ a **state-of-the-art** facility  
> ✅ The facility is **state of the art**.

副词 `-ly` 结尾 + 形容词的组合不用连字符：

> ✅ a highly successful author  
> ❌ a highly-successful author

### 用法二：复合数字与分数

21 至 99 的拼写数字：

> ✅ twenty-one, thirty-five, ninety-nine

分数作形容词时用连字符：

> ✅ a **two-thirds** majority  
> ✅ Two thirds of the students agreed.　（作名词时可不用）

### 用法三：前缀

大多数前缀与词根直接相连，不用连字符：

> ✅ unhappy, preview, cooperate, nonstop

以下情形用连字符：

**前缀 + 专有名词**

> ✅ pre-World War II, anti-American, post-Renaissance

**前缀 + 以相同字母开头的词根**（避免字母相撞）

> ✅ re-elect, anti-inflammatory, co-owner

**self- 前缀**

> ✅ self-confidence, self-evident, self-employed

**ex- 表示"前任"**

> ✅ ex-wife, ex-president

### 用法四：避免歧义

有无连字符可能改变词义：

|无连字符|有连字符|
|---|---|
|recover（康复）|re-cover（重新覆盖）|
|resign（辞职）|re-sign（重新签署）|
|recreation（娱乐）|re-creation（再创造）|

### 用法五：换行

单词在行末被迫分开时，在音节边界处加连字符。现代电子排版中此用法基本消失，但手写作文仍适用。

## 7.8 失误速查

|错例|问题|合法改写|
|---|---|---|
|I recieved the letter.|ei/ie 规则|I **received** the letter.|
|She is studing now.|词尾 y 规则|She is **studying** now.|
|He stoped the car.|双写末尾辅音|He **stopped** the car.|
|She spoke basicly.|-ic + -ally|She spoke **basically**.|
|The meeting is on tuesday.|星期名大写|The meeting is on **Tuesday**.|
|i study english.|句首大写 + 语言名大写|**I** study **English**.|
|She has 3 brothers.|小数字拼写|She has **three** brothers.|
|15 students passed.|句首数字拼写|**Fifteen** students passed.|
|The cat licked it's paws.|its 误作 it's|The cat licked **its** paws.|
|She is taller then I am.|than 误作 then|She is taller **than** I am.|
|The weather effects my mood.|affect 误作 effect|The weather **affects** my mood.|
|I read "Pride and Prejudice" last week.|长篇作品用斜体|I read _Pride and Prejudice_ last week.|
|A well known author arrived.|复合定语前置需连字符|A **well-known** author arrived.|
|The author is well-known.|复合定语后置不用连字符|The author is **well known**.|
|A highly-successful author.|-ly 副词不用连字符|A **highly successful** author.|
|She moved to the U.K. in Dec.|正文中国名、月份不缩写|She moved to the **United Kingdom** in **December**.|
|He earned his Ph.D..|句末缩写句号不重复|He earned his **Ph.D.**|
