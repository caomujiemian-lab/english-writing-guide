# 附录 A. 常见失误速查总表

本附录汇总各章中出现的高频错误类型，按章节编号组织。每一条包含一个典型错例、修改方案与错误机制说明。错例取材于专四写作中反复出现的错误模式，涉及中文表达迁移、语法概念混淆、规则记忆偏差三类来源。

---

## 第 1 章 句子骨架

### A1.1 介词短语或动名词短语被当作主语

> ❌ By doing exercises every day can keep us healthy.  
> ✅ Doing exercises every day can keep us healthy.  
> ✅ By doing exercises every day, we can keep ourselves healthy.

**机制**：`by + doing` 是介词短语，在句中充当状语，不能充当主语。对应中文句式"通过做运动可以保持健康"在英文中需要另外补出主语。

**修改方向**：去掉介词让动名词短语充当主语，或保留介词短语作状语并补出主句主语。

### A1.2 缺少谓语动词

> ❌ Many college students nowadays under great pressure from study and future employment.  
> ✅ Many college students nowadays **are** under great pressure from study and future employment.

**机制**：`under great pressure` 是介词短语作表语，需要系动词 `are` 才能构成 SVC 结构。主语被长修饰语拉开后，谓语容易被遗漏。

### A1.3 系动词后误用副词作表语

> ❌ The problem becomes seriously in recent years.  
> ✅ The problem becomes **serious** in recent years.

**机制**：系动词 `become / be / seem / look / feel` 后接主语补语（subject complement），由形容词或名词担任，不能由副词担任。

### A1.4 两个谓语动词并列而无连接

> ❌ Many students spend too much time on their phones forget the importance of face-to-face communication.  
> ✅ Many students spend too much time on their phones **and** forget the importance of face-to-face communication.  
> ✅ Many students **who** spend too much time on their phones forget the importance of face-to-face communication.

**机制**：一个简单句只能有一个谓语。两个动作并列需并列连词连接；若两者是修饰关系，则其中之一应降为定语从句或分词短语。

## 第 2 章 主谓一致

### A2.1 主语中心词被修饰语遮蔽

> ❌ The number of students who fail the exam every year are increasing.  
> ✅ The number of students who fail the exam every year **is** increasing.

**机制**：主语中心词是单数 `the number`，而非紧挨谓语的复数 `students`。

**易混对比**：

- `the number of + 复数名词` 意为"……的数量"，谓语用单数
- `a number of + 复数名词` 意为"许多"，谓语用复数

### A2.2 就近原则未使用

> ❌ Neither the teacher nor the students was satisfied with the result.  
> ✅ Neither the teacher nor the students **were** satisfied with the result.

**机制**：`neither...nor / either...or / not only...but also` 连接的主语，谓语与靠近谓语的主语一致。此处靠近谓语的是复数 `students`。

### A2.3 集合名词的数判断错误

> ❌ The police is investigating the case.  
> ✅ The police **are** investigating the case.

**机制**：`police` 在英语中总作复数处理，指向"警察这一群体中的成员"。同类词：`people`（表"人们"义时）、`cattle`。

### A2.4 "There be" 与后续名词数不一致

> ❌ There is many reasons for this phenomenon.  
> ✅ There **are** many reasons for this phenomenon.

**机制**：`there be` 结构中，`be` 的数由后面的真正主语决定。此处真正主语是复数 `reasons`。

### A2.5 名词性从句作主语被误判为复数

> ❌ What he said about the future of AI are shocking.  
> ✅ What he said about the future of AI **is** shocking.

**机制**：名词性主语从句视为单数，与从句内部名词的数无关。

## 第 3 章 三类致命句子错误

### A3.1 破句：从句被当作完整句子

> ❌ Many students choose to study abroad. Because they want to experience a different culture.  
> ✅ Many students choose to study abroad **because** they want to experience a different culture.  
> ✅ Many students choose to study abroad. **The main reason is that** they want to experience a different culture.

**机制**：`because` 引导的是从属分句，不能独立成句。

**修改方向**：与主句合并，或改写为独立分句。

### A3.2 破句：非谓语短语被当作完整句子

> ❌ The Internet has changed our lives in many ways. Making information easier to access.  
> ✅ The Internet has changed our lives in many ways, **making** information easier to access.  
> ✅ The Internet has changed our lives in many ways. **It has made** information easier to access.

**机制**：现在分词短语 `making ...` 无独立的主谓结构。

**修改方向**：用逗号连回主句，或补出主语与助动词使其独立成句。

### A3.3 逗号粘连：两独立分句用逗号连接

> ❌ Online learning is convenient, it also has many disadvantages.  
> ✅ Online learning is convenient**;** it also has many disadvantages.  
> ✅ Online learning is convenient**, but** it also has many disadvantages.  
> ✅ **Although** online learning is convenient, it also has many disadvantages.

**机制**：两独立分句之间必须以分号、逗号加并列连词、或从属连词重新组织，不能仅用逗号。

### A3.4 逗号粘连：连接副词被当作并列连词

> ❌ Online learning is convenient, however, it also has many disadvantages.  
> ✅ Online learning is convenient**;** however, it also has many disadvantages.  
> ✅ Online learning is convenient. **However,** it also has many disadvantages.

**机制**：`however / therefore / moreover / thus / furthermore` 是连接副词（conjunctive adverb），本身不承担连接两个独立分句的句法功能，因此两独立分句之间必须另有合法连接方式。

### A3.5 粘连句：两独立分句无任何连接

> ❌ The environment is getting worse we should take action immediately.  
> ✅ The environment is getting worse**, so** we should take action immediately.  
> ✅ **Since** the environment is getting worse, we should take action immediately.

**机制**：两独立分句之间若无任何标点或连接词，构成粘连句（run-on / fused sentence）。

## 第 4 章 并列

### A4.1 平行结构失衡：动词形式不一致

> ❌ In my spare time, I enjoy reading novels, listening to music, and to watch movies.  
> ✅ In my spare time, I enjoy **reading** novels, **listening** to music, and **watching** movies.

**机制**：`enjoy` 后接动名词，三个并列成分应保持同一形式。

### A4.2 平行结构失衡：词性不一致

> ❌ She is intelligent, hardworking, and has kindness.  
> ✅ She is intelligent, hardworking, and **kind**.

**机制**：并列成分应在同一语法层级。前两项为形容词，第三项若改为"动词 + 名词"即破坏平行。

### A4.3 关联连词两侧成分不对称

> ❌ The program not only helps students improve their English but also their critical thinking.  
> ✅ The program helps students improve not only **their English** but also **their critical thinking**.  
> ✅ The program not only **helps students improve their English** but also **develops their critical thinking**.

**机制**：`not only ... but also ...` 两侧的成分必须在语法层级上对称。原句中 `not only` 后接动词短语，`but also` 后只有名词短语，两端不平衡。

### A4.4 连接副词当并列连词使用

参见 [A3.4](#a34-%E9%80%97%E5%8F%B7%E7%B2%98%E8%BF%9E%E8%BF%9E%E6%8E%A5%E5%89%AF%E8%AF%8D%E8%A2%AB%E5%BD%93%E4%BD%9C%E5%B9%B6%E5%88%97%E8%BF%9E%E8%AF%8D)。

### A4.5 并列连词与从属连词同现

> ❌ He studies hard every day, and because he wants to get a good job after graduation.  
> ✅ He studies hard every day **because** he wants to get a good job after graduation.

**机制**：`and` 连接同级成分，`because` 引导从属分句，两者句法功能重叠。保留其一即可。

## 第 5 章 从属

### A5.1 although 与 but 连用

> ❌ Although he worked very hard, but he failed the exam.  
> ✅ **Although** he worked very hard, he failed the exam.  
> ✅ He worked very hard, **but** he failed the exam.

**机制**：中文"虽然……但是……"是两个词同现。英文中 `although` 为从属连词、`but` 为并列连词，两者只能保留一个。

### A5.2 because 与 so 连用

> ❌ Because he was ill, so he didn't come to class.  
> ✅ **Because** he was ill, he didn't come to class.  
> ✅ He was ill, **so** he didn't come to class.

**机制**：同 A5.1，中文"因为……所以……"在英文中不可同现。

### A5.3 垂悬分词：逻辑主语与主句主语不一致

> ❌ Walking along the campus road, a beautiful scene appeared in front of me.  
> ✅ Walking along the campus road, **I saw** a beautiful scene in front of me.

**机制**：`walking` 的逻辑主语应与主句主语一致。原句主句主语是 `a beautiful scene`，与"走路"这一动作不匹配。

**修改方向**：改写主句，使主句主语与分词逻辑主语一致。

### A5.4 垂悬不定式

> ❌ To improve our English, a lot of practice is needed.  
> ✅ To improve our English, **we need** a lot of practice.

**机制**：`to improve` 的逻辑主语应是施动者，与 `a lot of practice` 不匹配。

### A5.5 定语从句关系词与从句内代词重复

> ❌ This is the book which I bought it yesterday.  
> ✅ This is the book **which** I bought yesterday.  
> ✅ This is the book **that** I bought yesterday.

**机制**：关系代词 `which / that / who` 本身承担被修饰名词在从句中的成分，从句内不再出现指代同一对象的代词。

### A5.6 限定性与非限定性定语从句混淆

> ❌ My father who is a doctor works in Beijing.（在"只有一位父亲"的语境下）  
> ✅ My father**,** who is a doctor**,** works in Beijing.

**机制**：限定性定语从句用于从同类对象中区分具体对象；非限定性定语从句提供补充说明，两侧加逗号。当被修饰对象本身唯一时，应使用非限定形式。

### A5.7 that 引导非限定性定语从句

> ❌ The Great Wall, that attracts millions of tourists every year, is a symbol of China.  
> ✅ The Great Wall, **which** attracts millions of tourists every year, is a symbol of China.

**机制**：`that` 只能引导限定性定语从句。

## 第 6 章 标点符号

### A6.1 从句后置时误加逗号

> ❌ I stayed at home, because it was raining.  
> ✅ I stayed at home because it was raining.

**机制**：状语从句前置于主句时用逗号分隔；后置于主句时不加逗号。

### A6.2 主语与谓语之间误加逗号

> ❌ Students who spend too much time on their phones, often ignore their studies.  
> ✅ Students who spend too much time on their phones often ignore their studies.

**机制**：主语（含其限定修饰成分）与谓语之间不可用逗号切断。限定性定语从句属于主语的一部分，两侧不加逗号。

### A6.3 冒号前非独立分句

> ❌ The reasons are: economic, cultural, and personal.  
> ✅ The reasons are economic, cultural, and personal.  
> ✅ There are three reasons: economic, cultural, and personal.

**机制**：冒号引出列表时，冒号前须是独立分句。`The reasons are` 缺少表语，不是独立分句。

### A6.4 引号与句末标点相对位置

> ❌ He said, "I will come tomorrow".  
> ✅ He said, "I will come tomorrow**.**"

**机制**：美式英语规范中，句号与逗号置于引号内部。

### A6.5 分号两侧非独立分句

> ❌ Because of the heavy rain; we cancelled the trip.  
> ✅ Because of the heavy rain, we cancelled the trip.

**机制**：分号连接的两侧必须都是独立分句。`Because of the heavy rain` 是介词短语，不能独立成句。

## 第 7 章 卷面规范

### A7.1 专有名词未大写

> ❌ I have been studying english for ten years.  
> ✅ I have been studying **English** for ten years.

**机制**：语言名、国家名、民族名、月份、星期、节日首字母须大写。

### A7.2 句首未大写

> ❌ nowadays, more and more students choose to study abroad.  
> ✅ **Nowadays**, more and more students choose to study abroad.

### A7.3 同音异形词混淆

> ❌ Its important to keep a balance between study and rest.  
> ✅ **It's** important to keep a balance between study and rest.

**机制**：`its` 是物主代词"它的"；`it's` 是 `it is` 的缩写形式。

**其他高频混淆对**：

- `their`（他们的）/ `there`（那里）/ `they're`（他们是）
- `affect`（动词：影响）/ `effect`（名词：影响；动词：使发生）
- `then`（然后）/ `than`（比）
- `whose`（谁的）/ `who's`（who is）
- `lose`（动词：失去）/ `loose`（形容词：松的）

### A7.4 常见拼写错误

以下词在写作中出现误拼的频率较高：

- `environment` 误为 `enviroment`
- `government` 误为 `goverment`
- `receive` 误为 `recieve`
- `occurred` 误为 `occured`（末尾辅音需双写）
- `beginning` 误为 `begining`
- `believe` 误为 `beleive`
- `successful` 误为 `sucessful`
- `necessary` 误为 `neccessary` 或 `necesary`
- `separate` 误为 `seperate`

### A7.5 缩写形式用于正式作文

> ❌ There're lots of reasons why students can't find a good job.  
> ✅ **There are** lots of reasons why students **cannot** find a good job.

**机制**：专四写作属正式文体，`can't / won't / don't / it's / there're` 等缩写形式应改为完整形式。

### A7.6 数字书写不规范

> ❌ 20 students took part in the activity, 15 of them were girls.  
> ✅ **Twenty** students took part in the activity, **and fifteen** of them were girls.

**机制**：一百以下的整数通常拼写为英文单词；句首不得以阿拉伯数字开头。原句同时存在逗号粘连（见 A3.3），需一并修改。

### A7.7 复合定语位于名词前未用连字符

> ❌ This is a well known writer.  
> ✅ This is a **well-known** writer.

**机制**：复合形容词作前置定语时用连字符连接；作表语时不加连字符：`The writer is well known.`

## 综合失误：多错并存

一个长句中可能同时包含多类错误。下例为典型综合错例：

> ❌ Although the internet has brought us many convenience, but many students spending too much time online, it has a bad affect on their studies.

错误识别：

1. `although ... but` 同现 —— A5.1
2. `convenience` 为不可数名词，不可被 `many` 修饰 —— 应为 `much convenience` 或 `many conveniences`
3. `many students spending` 缺少谓语 —— A1.2
4. `many students spending ..., it has ...` 两独立分句仅用逗号连接 —— A3.3
5. `affect` 误用为名词 —— A7.3
6. `internet` 首字母未大写 —— A7.1

修改：

> ✅ Although the Internet has brought us much convenience, many students spend too much time online, which has a bad effect on their studies.
