**并列**（coordination）指用连接手段把两个或多个**语法地位相同**的成分放在同一层次上。被连接的可以是单词、短语，也可以是独立分句。与之对应的是**从属**（subordination），把信息分出主次。这部分在第 5 章处理。

并列涉及三类连接手段：**并列连词**、**关联连词**、**连接副词**。三者在词性、位置、标点上各不相同，混用是专四改错与写作的高频失分点。此外，并列结构中的各成分必须满足**平行**（parallelism），也就是语法形式一致。

- 连接副词 however 族

## 4.1 并列连词：FANBOYS

英语中的**并列连词**（coordinating conjunctions）共七个，首字母缩写为 **FANBOYS**：

|连词|逻辑关系|示例|
|---|---|---|
|**F**or|原因（= because，较正式）|He stayed home, for it was raining.|
|**A**nd|并列、递进|She speaks English and French.|
|**N**or|否定并列|He doesn't smoke, nor does he drink.|
|**B**ut|转折|The task was hard but rewarding.|
|**O**r|选择|You can take the bus or the subway.|
|**Y**et|转折（较正式）|She was exhausted, yet she kept working.|
|**S**o|结果|It was raining, so we stayed home.|

### 连接单词或短语

连接两个并列成分时通常不加逗号：

> ✅ bread **and** butter  
> ✅ slowly **but** surely  
> ✅ to study **or** to work

连接三个及以上并列成分时，成分之间用逗号，最后一个成分前的逗号（牛津逗号）可加可不加，保持一致即可：

> ✅ red, white, and blue  
> ✅ red, white and blue

### 连接两个独立分句

连接两个独立分句时，并列连词前加逗号：

> ✅ It was raining**, so** we stayed home.  
> ✅ She studied hard**, but** she failed the exam.  
> ✅ You can take the bus**, or** you can walk.

### nor 后的倒装

否定句后用 nor 连接另一个否定分句时，nor 后的分句使用**部分倒装**（助动词前置）：

> ✅ He doesn't smoke, **nor does he** drink.  
> ✅ She didn't attend the meeting, **nor did she** send a representative.

## 4.2 关联连词：成对使用

**关联连词**（correlative conjunctions）成对出现，分别置于被连接成分之前。

|关联连词|含义|示例|
|---|---|---|
|both ... and ...|两者都|The novel is **both** entertaining **and** thought-provoking.|
|either ... or ...|二者之一|You can **either** stay **or** leave.|
|neither ... nor ...|两者都不|He is **neither** rich **nor** famous.|
|not only ... but also ...|不仅……而且……|She is **not only** a teacher **but also** a writer.|
|not ... but ...|不是……而是……|The problem is **not** difficult **but** tedious.|
|whether ... or ...|是……还是……|**Whether** you agree **or** disagree, the decision stands.|

### 关联连词的主谓一致

`either...or / neither...nor / not only...but also` 连接的主语采用**就近一致**——谓语与靠近它的那个主语保持一致：

> ✅ Neither the students **nor the teacher was** aware of the change.  
> ✅ Neither the teacher **nor the students were** aware of the change.

`both...and` 连接的主语视为复数：

> ✅ Both the teacher and the students **were** present.

详见[第 2 章 主谓一致](ch2-subject-verb-agreement)。

### 关联连词与平行结构

关联连词两侧的成分必须语法形式一致。详见 4.5 节。

## 4.3 连接副词：词性是副词

**连接副词**（conjunctive adverbs）在语义上表达逻辑过渡，但词性是副词，不是连词。

|逻辑关系|连接副词|
|---|---|
|递进|moreover, furthermore, besides, in addition, also|
|转折|however, nevertheless, nonetheless, on the contrary, on the other hand|
|结果|therefore, thus, hence, consequently, accordingly, as a result|
|对比|in contrast, conversely, instead|
|举例|for example, for instance|
|总结|in short, in conclusion, in summary|
|时间|meanwhile, subsequently, afterwards|

### 与独立分句的连接规则

连接副词**不能**单独连接两个独立分句。合法的搭配方式有三种：

**句首——前加分号，后加逗号**

> ✅ It was raining**; however,** we went out anyway.  
> ✅ He studied hard**; therefore,** he passed the exam.

**句中——两侧加逗号**

> ✅ It was raining; we**, however,** went out anyway.  
> ✅ He studied hard; he**, therefore,** passed the exam.

**句末——前加逗号**

> ✅ It was raining; we went out anyway**, however.**

这三种方式都基于一个前提：**分号**（或句号）完成两分句的连接，连接副词只负责标示逻辑关系。

### 违反规则的典型错误

> ❌ It was raining, however, we went out anyway.  
> ❌ He studied hard, therefore, he passed the exam.

两例均为逗号粘连（见[第 3 章](ch3-sentence-errors)）。`however` / `therefore` 的词性是副词，不能像 `but` / `so` 那样以逗号连接独立分句。

## 4.4 三类连接手段对比

|维度|并列连词（FANBOYS）|关联连词（either...or 族）|连接副词（however 族）|
|---|---|---|---|
|词性|连词|连词（成对）|副词|
|连接独立分句|✅|✅（连接分句时按并列连词规则）|❌（需借助分号）|
|位置|两分句之间，固定|成对分置两成分前|句首、句中、句末均可|
|两独立分句之间的标点|`S + V**,** and S + V.`|`S + V**,** but also S + V.`|`S + V**;** however**,** S + V.`|
|典型误用|—|两侧成分不平行|被当作并列连词，构成逗号粘连|

连接两个独立分句时标点模板的对比：

> ✅ It was raining**, so** we stayed home.　（FANBOYS）  
> ✅ It was raining**; therefore,** we stayed home.　（连接副词）  
> ❌ It was raining**, therefore,** we stayed home.　（连接副词被误作并列连词）

## 4.5 平行结构

**平行结构**（parallelism）指并列的各成分在语法形式上保持一致——同为名词、同为动名词、同为不定式、同为介词短语、同为从句等。

### 单词层面

> ❌ She is **smart**, **kind**, and **a hard worker**.  
> ✅ She is **smart**, **kind**, and **hardworking**.  
> ✅ She is **a smart**, **kind**, and **hardworking** person.

三个并列成分应同为形容词，或同为"冠词 + 形容词 + 名词"。

### 短语层面

> ❌ She enjoys **reading**, **swimming**, and **to cook**.  
> ✅ She enjoys **reading**, **swimming**, and **cooking**.

三个成分应同为动名词。

> ❌ The manager told us **to arrive early**, **to dress formally**, and **that we should bring ID**.  
> ✅ The manager told us **to arrive early**, **to dress formally**, and **to bring ID**.

三个成分应同为不定式短语。

### 从句层面

> ❌ What he said and **his actions** contradict each other.  
> ✅ **What he said** and **what he did** contradict each other.

两个成分应同为名词性从句。

### 关联连词的平行

关联连词两侧必须平行——两侧放什么，另一侧就放什么。

> ❌ She is **not only** beautiful **but also** she is intelligent.  
> ✅ She is **not only** beautiful **but also** intelligent.

> ❌ You can **either** call me **or** you can send an email.  
> ✅ You can **either** call me **or** send me an email.  
> ✅ **Either** you call me **or** you send me an email.

> ❌ The policy is **both** efficient **and** it saves money.  
> ✅ The policy is **both** efficient **and** cost-saving.

### 比较结构中的平行

`more ... than ... / as ... as ...` 两侧的成分也应平行：

> ❌ Reading a novel is more enjoyable than **to watch a film**.  
> ✅ Reading a novel is more enjoyable than **watching a film**.

> ❌ Writing an essay is as challenging as **to solve** a math problem.  
> ✅ Writing an essay is as challenging as **solving** a math problem.

## 4.6 失误速查

|错例|问题|合法改写|
|---|---|---|
|It was raining, however, we went out.|连接副词被当作并列连词|It was raining**;** however, we went out.|
|He studied hard, therefore he passed.|同上|He studied hard**;** therefore, he passed.|
|She enjoys reading, swimming, and to cook.|平行失衡（动名词 vs 不定式）|She enjoys reading, swimming, and **cooking**.|
|She is not only beautiful but also she is intelligent.|关联连词两侧不平行|She is not only beautiful but also **intelligent**.|
|Either you apologize or I will leave.|合法|—|
|Either you apologize or leaving is my choice.|关联连词两侧不平行|**Either you apologize or I will leave.**|
|Neither the students nor the teacher were aware.|就近一致|Neither the students nor the teacher **was** aware.|
|He doesn't smoke, nor he drinks.|nor 后未倒装|He doesn't smoke, nor **does he drink**.|
|Reading a novel is more enjoyable than to watch a film.|比较结构不平行|Reading a novel is more enjoyable than **watching** a film.|
|The report is clear, concise, and it is informative.|平行失衡|The report is clear, concise, and **informative**.|
